Calendar connection

Google Calendar

Allows Calendly to check Google calendar for conflicts and add events to it.

Office 365/Outlook.com

Allows Calendly to check your Office 365, Outlook.com, live.com, or hotmail calendar for conflicts
and add events to it.

Outlook Plug-In (for Windows users)

Allows Calendly to check Outlook for conflicts and add events to it.
Requirements: Outlook 2007, 2010, 2013 or 2016 with Windows XP, Vista, 7, 8, 8.1 or 10.

iCloud Calendar

Allows Calendly to check your iCloud calendar for conflicts and add events to it.
